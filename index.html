Simple Omelet Recipe

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tablespoon of milk
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 tablespoon of butter
  • Optional: cheese, vegetables, or herbs

Instructions

  1. In a bowl, whisk together the eggs, milk, salt, and pepper.
  2. Heat the butter in a non-stick skillet over medium heat.
  3. Pour the egg mixture into the skillet and cook until the edges start to set.
  4. Add any optional ingredients on one half of the omelet.
  5. Fold the omelet in half and cook for another minute.
  6. Serve hot and enjoy!
